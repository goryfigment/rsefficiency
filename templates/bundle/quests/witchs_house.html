Witch's House

Requirements:

  • Defeat Skavid (level 19)
  • Defeat Spider (level 30)
  • Defeat Bear (level 42)
  • Defeat Wolf (level 53)

Items:

  • Cheese
  • Leather gloves

Recommended:

  • Mage or range weapons
  • Food

Getting into the Witch's garden.

  • Talk to the Boy. (Chat: 1-1)
    East of Taverly
    by the hedge garden
  • Search the northern potted plank for a key and enter.
    Witch's house entrance
    north of Taverly.
    Right-click the potted plant, and select 'Look under'.
  • Search the cupboard to get a Magnet. (Equip Leather gloves)
    Inside witch's house
    at the northern room
    Go downstairs into the basement.
    Basement
    Getting leather gloves.
    If you don't have leather gloves, search the boxes.
    Basement
    The gate.
    You must wear gloves to enter the gate.
    Basement
    The cupboard.
    Search the cupboard to get a Magnet.
  • Pickup and read the diary.
    Witch's house main room
    on the table.
    Read all the pages of the diary.
  • Drop the cheese and use the magnet on the mouse.
    Witch's house south room
    by the mouse hole.
    Drop the cheese in the southern room,
    then use the cheese on the mouse by the mouse hole.

  • Search the fountain for a key.
    Witch's garden,
    north side
    Go around the garden avoiding the witch's view,
    if you get caught you will need another cheese to get back.
